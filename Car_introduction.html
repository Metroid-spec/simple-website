Arduino Smart Car Introduction


The figure above is a smart car build using an arduino uno,
and other components. The main concept behind the car is
that it will move continously move foward until it detects and
object infront of it. If the the car detects an object 10cm
infront of it, it will :

  1. Come to a stop
  2. Reverse
  3. Check right for objects
  4. Check left for objects
  5. Turn left or right after exploring its environment
